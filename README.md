# Car Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing car sales data to evaluate overall sales performance, pricing trends, and regional contribution. The dashboard is designed using Power BI to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Objective
The objective of this project is to design and develop an interactive Car Sales Dashboard that enables stakeholders to:
- Monitor sales performance over time
- Compare current performance with previous periods
- Identify trends, patterns, and growth opportunities
- Support strategic and operational decision-making

---

## Key Objectives
- Analyze Year-to-Date (YTD), Month-to-Date (MTD), and Year-over-Year (YOY) sales performance  
- Track average selling price trends over time  
- Understand product preferences across body styles and colors  
- Evaluate regional sales performance across dealer locations  
- Provide both summary-level insights and detailed sales analysis  

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- YTD Total Sales  
- MTD Total Sales  
- YOY Growth in Sales  
- Average Selling Price (YTD & MTD)  
- YOY Growth in Average Price  
- Cars Sold (YTD & MTD)  
- YOY Growth in Cars Sold  

---

## Analysis Covered
- Sales trend analysis to identify growth patterns and seasonality  
- Average price analysis to understand pricing behavior  
- Product-level analysis based on body style and color  
- Regional sales performance across dealer regions  
- Company-wise contribution to total revenue and sales volume  
- Detailed transaction-level sales analysis  

---

## Visualizations Used
- Line charts for weekly and time-based sales trends  
- Pie charts for sales distribution by body style and color  
- Map visualization for regional sales analysis  
- Tabular grids for company-wise and detailed sales information  

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset used in this project is a **sample / anonymized car sales dataset**.  
It contains information related to:
- Car models and attributes  
- Sales amount and average price  
- Dealer regions  
- Sales dates and transaction details  

No confidential or real customer data is used.
